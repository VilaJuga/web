## What They Do (Summary)

Vilajuga is a board game festival initiative in Vilafranca del Penedes (Catalonia), organized by the non-profit association La Casa dels Estranys. Their mission is to bring tabletop gaming culture to all audiences through open play spaces, tournaments, role-playing games, family zones, demonstrations, and community events.

They run year-round collaboration activities in the Penedes area and organize the annual Vilajuga event at the Fira pavilion in Vilafranca del Penedes.

### FESTIVAL DEL JOC DEL PENEDES VILAJUGA

Original (Catalan):
"Es un festival destinat a acostar els jocs de taula, jocs de rol, jocs familiars i aquelles activitats mes mogudes a tot el public, a Vilafranca del Penedes."

English meaning:
A festival designed to bring board games, role-playing games, family games, and more active game activities to all audiences in Vilafranca del Penedes.

### Què hi trobarem? (What will we find?)

#### Ludoteca pròpia (Own game library)
- They have a library of 300+ board games across categories and styles.
- Visitors can request game titles or get recommendations from volunteers.
- Games are played at many prepared tables during the event.

#### Activitats preparades per a totes les edats (Activities for all ages)
- Includes board/card game tournaments.
- Includes spaces prepared for full-family participation.
- Different zones are set up for a complete game-focused day.

#### Demostracions de jocs (Game demonstrations)
- Demonstrations of published games by collaborating publishers.
- Demonstrations of unpublished prototypes explained by their authors.

### Què s'hi inclou? (What is included?)
- Free-access game library (300+ board games).
- Free board-game play using their library.
- Guidance from organization members on what to play.
- Demonstrations of published and prototype games.
- Wargames / strategy miniature war-game experiences.
- Role-playing games led by collaborators and game masters.
- "Jocs de NO taula" area (non-table game activities).
- Family game zone for younger children, including motor/sensory exploration.

## Page: Sobre Nosaltres (About Us)
Source: https://www.vilajuga.org/about-us/

### Qui som? (Who are we?)
- They are "la Casa dels Estranys," a non-profit association.
- Founded in 1994 by people united by playing board and role-playing games.
- During the year, they collaborate with other entities in the Penedes region through smaller activities.
- Once a year, they celebrate the larger Vilajuga board-game event at the Vilafranca Fira pavilion.

### Historical Background
- Early format was called "Juguem!"
- Organized jointly by La Casa dels Estranys and ADALT.
- First edition was in 2008.
- After 8 editions, in 2016 (with generational organizer transition), they adopted the current format/name: Vilajuga.

## Consolidated Purpose Statement

Vilajuga and la Casa dels Estranys focus on growing local tabletop culture by:
- Running an accessible annual festival with open game access.
- Organizing tournaments and demonstrations (published games and prototypes).
- Supporting role-play and strategy/wargame communities.
- Offering family-friendly spaces for all ages.
- Building volunteer-driven community activities throughout the year.
